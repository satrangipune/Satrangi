A Few Words About Us

We strive to create a more inclusive space to express and be yourself.

There is a need to create a more inclusive space on campus for students who do not necessarily fit into conventional societal definitions of gender and sexuality. Thus, Satrangi, an LGBTQIA+ club, was setup to address the many issues that those on the spectrum face.

We intend to not only create a safe space of tolerance, acceptance and inclusion, but also change mindsets and erase the stigma that plague gender and sexual minorities.

The Club also organises events in IISER Pune Campus and outside the campus to raise awareness and empower those on the spectrum such as the LGBTQIA+ Pride Parade organised in collaboration with the Social Initiatives Department of Karavaan in October 2019.

IISER Pune LGBTQIA+ Support Group

Satrangi will be hosting regular informal sessions in IISER where people share their stories and express themselves with no fear of discrimination and judgement. These sessions will be Open to All and anyone is welcome to share their stories.

Our Mission: To Empower and Raise Awareness

The aims of Satrangi are as follows:

  • To create a sense of community, acceptance and inclusivity for those on the spectrum.
  • To provide a platform for safe exploration and expression of identity.
  • To create awareness regarding sexual and gender minorities and tackle the problems they face.
  • To organise outreach programmes that foster support and allyship.
  • To organise events and workshops that explore culture through the queer lens
